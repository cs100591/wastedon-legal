Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

WastedOn ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Spending habits: The habit names and amounts you enter are stored locally on your device and synced to our servers for backup and cross-device access.
  • Budget settings: Your weekly budget preferences.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Purchase information: We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat collects transaction data necessary to manage your subscription. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Anonymous identifiers: A randomly generated anonymous ID is used to manage your subscription status. We do not collect your Apple ID or personal identity.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the app's core features (habit tracking, statistics, AI-generated reports)
  • To process and manage your subscription
  • To generate AI-powered spending analysis (your spending data is sent to our secure server for processing)

Data Storage and Security

Your spending data is stored locally on your device and backed up to secure cloud servers (Supabase). Data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Subscription and purchase management
  • Supabase: Secure cloud data storage
  • Cloudflare Workers: AI report generation

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above, solely to provide app functionality.

Your Rights

  • You can delete all your data by deleting the app
  • You can request data deletion by contacting us
  • You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings

Children's Privacy

WastedOn is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
